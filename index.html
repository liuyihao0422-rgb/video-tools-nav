💡 Pro Tips

🗜️ Compress Without Quality Loss

Use H.264 codec for best compatibility. For web, keep bitrate under 5Mbps for 1080p video.

📱 Social Media Formats

TikTok/Reels: 9:16 aspect ratio, 1080x1920. YouTube: 16:9, 1920x1080. Instagram: 1:1 or 4:5.

⚡ Fast Conversion Tips

Choose MP4 format with H.264 codec for fastest conversion speed and best compatibility.

🎬 Video Quality Guide

720p for social media, 1080p for YouTube, 4K for professional. Match export settings to your platform.
